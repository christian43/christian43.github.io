After studying sport science in Berlin and finishing my Ph.D. at the University Leipzig i work now as a research assistant in Leipzig. Get a short CV.

I am interested in analysing data from different sources and delivering data-driven decision support. Ideally these data sources are connected with sport areas such as performance, movements, training or competition. But does not have to. One of my favourite tool to solve statistical problems is R “The R Project for statistical computing”. Programming tools like Python or Julia are interesting and powerfull as well.

This website serves two purposes. First, it is my personal blog. Here i write post mainly for myself. Maybe like a Notebook just to remember things. Second, this website should also work like a business card where you can find out how to contact me.

As a sport scientist i try to combine two worlds. The world of training and competition and the world of research and data analytics.
