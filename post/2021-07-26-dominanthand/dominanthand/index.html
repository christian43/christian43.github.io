Simple Questions

I love simple questions were the answer is more complex than expected. This is the first post about such simple questions. One of these question was asked by my wife during the olympic games 2021.

Does it make a difference where the athletes have their oar in pairs rowing?

In other words can we swap the athlete´s boat position without negative consequences? Similiar we can ask if there are side differences or site preferences within an athlete.

Method

Beside aspects like training and practice another one comes ahead. Assumed that there is a difference between the force output of the dominant and the non-dominant arm, it could be an advantage to have the arm with greater force production at the outer end of the oar. Cause of the longer lever arm at the end of the oar.

First of all i do not know if this argumentation is conlusive. Second i do not know if there is a difference between the dominant and non-dominant arm in maximum force production in rowing even not in simple hand strength test procedures.
